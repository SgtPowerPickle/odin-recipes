Beef Pho

Description

This is a comforting bowl of rich, spiced beef broth ladled over rice noodles and thinly sliced beef.

Ingredients

  • Beef bones
  • Beef
  • Herbs and Seasonings
  • Rice Noodles
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Boil beef and bones and remove the scum.
  2. Toast onions, spices and ginger.
  3. Add toasted spices, beef, and beef bones to a large pot with water.
  4. Add salt and fish sauce and simmer for 2 hours.
  5. Cook rice noodles according to package directions.
  6. Build bowl of pho and laddle boiling broth over top.
  7. Serve with toppings.
