Roasted Chicken

Description

This juicy roasted chicken recipe is perfect for beginner cooks and old pros alike.

Ingredients

  • Whole 3lb chicken
  • Seasonings
  • Butter
  • Celery

Steps

  1. Season the chicken inside and out.
  2. Add butter to the chicken and pan.
  3. Stuff cavity of chicken with celery.
  4. Bake chicken in 350F oven until internal temperature in thickest part of thigh registers 165F.
